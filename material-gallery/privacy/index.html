Privacy policy

General

Material Gallery is developed by one developer. Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed. By using Material Gallery, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Material Gallery does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION` permission is used to retrieve unredacted Exif metadata from media

The `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is required on Android below 10 to access or remove photos and videos from the users' device.

The `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is used to access photos and videos from the users' device.

The `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`, `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO` permission is required on Android above 12 to access photos and videos from the users' device.

The `VIBRATE` permission is used to give haptic feedback to the user.
