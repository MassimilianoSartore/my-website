Privacy policy

General

Ares is developed by one developer. Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed. By using Ares, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Ares does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` permission is used to access information about networks

The `INTERNET` permission is used to open network sockets.

The `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` permission is used to keep the server up until you terminate the app.

The `CAMERA` permission is used to scan QR codes.

The `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` permission used on API 33 and higher to keep the server up until you terminate the app.
